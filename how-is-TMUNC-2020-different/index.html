How is TMUNC 2020 different?


SUBSTANTIVE EXCELLENCE

The secretariat of TMUNC 2020 is carefully selected to include the most passionate and dedicated members from all over the state. Each of the background guides is carefully crafted for its respective committee and reflects careful and thorough research. All of our executive board are extremely knowledgeable, dedicated, and committed to the ideals of TMUNC 2020 and the broader educational mission of Model United Nations, resulting in well-run and focused committees. Many delegates leave our conference with a new-found passion for international relations and greater awareness of the issues that dominate our world.
